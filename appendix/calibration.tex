
\chapter{ALPHA CALIBRATION ENERGIES}

A mixed \nuc{241}{Am}/\nuc{148}{Gd} alpha-particle source was used to
calibrate the Si strip detector and determine the \nuc{27}{Al} target
thickness. For the produced alphas during the experiment, they are
closer in energy to those from the decay of \nuc{148}{Gd}.
