uncertainty bounds when controllable uncertainties are artifically held
at zero.

In the tables below, the uncertainty bounds are given as a percent
confidence interval, where the lower (L) and upper (H) bounds are
presented alongside the total uncertainty width (W) for the given
percent interval.

All acceptances are given in mrad.
